What You Didn't Know

  • More than 3.5 million young adults aged 18 to 25 experience a form of homelessness in the course of one year -- that's 1 of every 10 young adults.
  • 1.2 million students in the U.S are homeless.
  • At least 700,000 adolescent minors ages 13 to 17 experience a form of homelessness in the course of one year -- that's 1 of every adolescent adults.
  • In total, at least 4.2 million young people experience a form of homelessness in a given year, from coach surfing to living on the streets.
  • Half of the youth who experienced homelessness in the past year did so for the first time.
  • LGBTQ youth are more than double the risk of homelessness than their non-LGBTQ peers, and had over twice the rate of early death among all youth experiencing homelessness.
  • Black or African American youth had an 83% higher risk of homelessness.
  • Hispanic, nonwhite youth had a 33% higher risk of homelessness.
  • Homelessness in general increased modestly in the United States in 2018, the second increase in two years.
